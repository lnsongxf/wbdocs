3. Recurrent Cost Financing
For FY1393 a ceiling for the Recurrent Cost Window (RCW) of US$400 million was set out in the
ARTF Financing Strategy 1391-1393. The ceiling is made up of US$125 million in baseline
financing, US$175 million for the Incentive Program (IP) and US$100 million for the Operations &
Maintenance (O&M) Facility.

At the end of FY1393 December 21, 2014, the FY1393 baseline financing of US$125 million was
fully disbursed. US$113.70 million was disbursed towards the IP (FY1392 US$48.70m & FY1393
US$75m) and US$33.90 million in O&M financing. Please see details in table 3 below.

Based on endorsement by the ARTF Steering Committee on December 1, 2013, a new arrangement
was introduced under the RCW: the “Ad Hoc Payments (AHP)”a. A total amount of US$146.96
million was disbursed under the AHP during FY1393.
