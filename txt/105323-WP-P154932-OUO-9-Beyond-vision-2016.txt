Introduction**
Botswana’s!Vision!2016!will!come!to!end,!after!20!years,!early!next!year.!The!process!of!
developing!a!new!national!Vision!to!cover!the!period!2016!–!2036!is!under!way.!It!is!
intended!that!the!new!Vision!will!include!a!monitoring!and!evaluation!framework.!As!
part!of!this,!the!draft!Framework!for!Vision!Beyond!2016!has!proposed!19!Final!
Outcome!Indicators!(FOIs).!This!report!supports!those!FOIs!by!providing!targets!for!this!
set!of!indicators,!which!can!be!used!to!monitor!progress!with!the!achievement!of!highL
level!Vision!Beyond!2016!objectives.!The!assignment!also!includes!the!generation!of!
Intermediate!Outcome!Indicators,!which!support!the!process!by!identifying!measurable!
subsidiary!indicators!that!will!contribute!to!the!achievement!of!the!FOIs.!
The!report!gives!definitions!for!each!FOI!and!IOI,!identifies!data!sources,!and!provides!
information!on!frequency,!availability!and!any!data!issues.!!
The!postL2016!Vision!framework!has!four!pillars:!WellLBeing!of!Citizens;!Human!
Development!Outcomes;!Economic!Growth!and!Job!Creation;!and!Sustainability!of!
Development.!The!report!is!structured!around!these!four!pillars,!with!the!relevant!FOIs!
and!IOS.!!!

Well.Being*of*Citizens*
FOI*#*1:*Headcount*Poverty*Rate*
Headcount!poverty!is!measured!relative!to!the!national!Poverty!Datum!Line!(PDL).!It!
has!declined!substantially,!from!59%!in!1985/6,!to!47%!in!1993/4,!31%!in!2002/3!and!
19%!in!2009/10.!Fitting!a!trend!to!this!rate!of!decline!indicates!that!the!poverty!rate!
declined,!on!average,!by!4.5%!(not!percentage!points)!a!year.!!
A!simple!approach!to!setting!a!target!is!to!assume!that!this!rate!of!decline!continues!
through!to!2036.!This!is!a!demanding!target,!because,!as!poverty!falls,!it!becomes!more!
and!more!difficult!to!reduce!poverty!further.!!
A!continuation!of!this!trend!leads!to!a!headcount!poverty!target!in!2036!of!6.3%!(i.e.!a!
reduction!of!around!twoLthirds!from!the!most!recent!measured!headcount!poverty!rate,!
from!the!2009/10!BCWIS).!!

FOI*#*3:*Gini*Coefficient*
The!Gini!coefficient!measures!the!degree!of!inequality!in!the!distribution!of!income!(or!
of!consumption!spending).!Data!on!the!Gini!coefficient!is!somewhat!inconsistent,!with!
Statistics!Botswana!(2013)!and!the!World!Bank!(2015)!reporting!different!results!even!
when!based!on!the!same!dataset,!due!to!different!methodologies2.!
The!World!Bank!reports!the!following!results!for!the!Gini:!2002/3!L!64.7!and!2009/10!L!
60.5,!thereby!indicating!a!reduction!in!inequality!between!the!two!surveys!(HIES!and!
BCWIS).!Nevertheless,!even!at!60.5!the!Gini!is!high!by!international!standards,!making!
Botswana!one!of!the!most!unequal!countries!in!the!world.!Here!we!use!the!World!Bank!
figures,!as!the!calculation!is!based!on!internationally!accepted!methodology.!

IOI:*Rate*of*Increase*of*the*Income*of*the*lowest*40%*of*the*population*
Inequality!can!only!be!reduced!by!faster!income!growth!for!lower!income!groups!than!

over!the!period!(while!overall!incomes!increase!by!138%).!!

FOI*#*5:*Life*Expectancy*at*Birth*
Life!expectancy!at!birth!(LEB)!is!one!of!the!most!important!indicators!of!the!quality!of!
life!as!it!encompasses!a!wide!range!of!human!development!conditions!(health,!education,!
safety,!nutrition!etc.).!In!Botswana’s!case!LEB!was!dramatically!and!negatively!impacted!

Since!that!time,!however,!it!has!increased!following!the!successful!rollLout!of!treatment.!!
Figures!from!different!sources!are!somewhat!contradictory,!especially!during!the!period!
of!maximum!HIV/AIDS!impact.!Statistics!Botswana!reports!LEB!of!68!years!as!at!the!
2011!Census.!The!UNDP!uses!LEB!as!one!of!the!components!of!the!Human!Development!
Index,!and!reports!a!figure!of!64.4!years!for!Botswana!in!2013.!!
It!is!proposed!that!a!target!be!set!for!life!expectancy!of!74!years!which!is!the!average!for!
“High!Human!Development”!countries!in!the!HDI.!!

for!health.!!!

IOI:*Infant*Mortality*

IOI:*Measures*of*Disease*Burden*
With!the!largely!successful!management!of!HIV/AIDS!and!consequent!reduction!in!death!
rates,!other!diseases!are!becoming!more!significant,!including!cancer!and!other!nonL
communicable!diseases.!It!may!be!worth!including!as!an!IOI!a!measure!of!other!disease!
burden,!including!malaria,!tuberculosis,!diabetes!and!cancer.!This!could!be!constructed!

    •   Cancer!cases!per!10,000!population!

specifically!provided.!

FOI*#*6:*HIV*Incidence*Rate*
Botswana!has!made!good!progress!in!dealing!with!the!HIV!epidemic.!AntiLretroviral!
Therapy!(ARV)!therapy!has!been!made!widely!available!through!the!public!health!
service,!and!death!rates!have!dropped!dramatically!as!survival!times!on!treatment!have!
increased.!High!levels!of!health!resources!have!been!made!available!to!deal!with!the!
epidemic.!!
Notwithstanding!these!achievements!–!or!indeed!partly!because!of!them!–!large!
numbers!of!people!in!Botswana!are!HIVLpositive.!The!HIV!prevalence!rate!has!remained!
high,!in!part!because!of!the!success!of!ARV!treatment!in!prolonging!life!expectancy.!
Because!of!the!extended!life!expectancy!of!HIV+!adults,!and!the!fact!that!they!will!remain!
infectious!forever,!it!will!take!a!long!time!to!achieve!freedom!from!HIV!infection.!In!this!
respect!HIV!infection!is!not!like,!for!instance,!the!Ebola!virus,!where!a!patient!is!no!
longer!infectious,!once!cured.!HIV!infection!is!not!cured,!simply!managed!and!
suppressed.!!
LongLterm!projections!of!HIV!incidence!(the!rate!of!new!infections)!and!prevalence!(the!
proportion!of!a!population!group!infected)!have!been!prepared!by!UNAIDS!using!the!
Spectrum!model.!The!UNAIDS!projections!include!incidence!and!prevalence!under!
different!scenarios!of!treatment!and!resource!allocation!for!HIV/AIDS!(for!instance,!
relating!to!resources!for!prevention,!and!eligibility!for!treatment).!!
The!current!(2015)!HIV!incidence!rate!is!estimated!at!1.09%.!The!UNAIDS!projections!
show!that!on!the!basis!of!“business!as!usual”,!i.e.!a!continuation!of!current!policies,!the!
HIV+!population!will!continue!to!rise!in!numerical!terms,!and!the!incidence!rate!will!
only!decline!slowly,!to!0.91%!in!2036.!An!enhanced!treatment!programme!along!with!
additional!resources!for!prevention!and!health!system!strengthening!will!lead!to!a!
steeper!decline,!to!0.35%!in!2036,!but!zero!incidence!will!not!be!achievable.!It!is!
proposed!that!this!figure!of!0.35%!be!adopted!as!the!FOI!target!for!2036.!

IOI:*HIV*Prevalence*Rate*
While!the!focus!is!rightly!on!incidence!(new!infections),!policy!should!also!aim!to!bring!
down!the!number!of!people!infected!and!the!adult!prevalence!rate.!The!same!UNAIDS!
projections!show!that!under!the!enhanced!scenario,!it!will!decline!from!23%!in!2015!to!
16%!in!2036.!It!is!proposed!that!16%!be!adopted!as!the!adult!prevalence!target!for!2036.!
This!may!seem!unduly!high,!but!it!is!important!to!remember!that!with!improved!
treatment,!many!people!who!are!HIV+!now!will!still!be!alive!in!2036.!!

FOI*#*9:*GDP*Growth*Rate*
One!of!the!aims!of!the!economic!growth!target!is!to!reach!“highLincome”!status!(as!per!
World!Bank!classification)!during!the!period!of!the!new!Vision.!The!current!
classification!used!by!the!World!Bank!is!that!a!highLincome!country!has!Gross!National!
Income!(GNI)!per!capita,!measured!as!per!the!WB’s!methodology,!of!at!least!$12,746.!
This!is!adjusted!each!year!to!keep!it!constant!in!real!terms,!i.e.!in!line!with!international!
inflation!(specify),!but!is!not!adjusted!to!reflect!general!economic!growth.!The!most!
recent!World!Bank!GNI!figure!for!Botswana!is!$7,730!(2013).!!
Hence!an!increase!of!65%!is!required!to!achieve!highLincome!status,!equivalent!to!2.1%!
a!year!over!the!period!to!2036!(20!years!from!the!start!of!the!new!Vision).!To!calculate!
the!desired!rate!of!economic!growth,!we!need!to!also!take!into!account!population!
growth.!Between!the!2001!and!2011!censuses!this!averaged!2%!a!year.!Population!
projections!from!the!UN!Population!Division!–!which!are!widely!accepted!as!a!reliable!
source!of!longLterm!global!population!projections!–!gives!a!population!growth!rate!of!
just!under!1%!a!year!for!Botswana!under!its!“medium!fertility”!projection!and!1.3%!in!
its!“high!fertility”!projection.!We!use!an!estimate!of!1.5%!a!year!for!mediumLterm!
population!growth.!
Hence!average!real!GDP!growth!of!3.7%!per!annum!would!be!necessary!to!achieve!highL
income!status!by!2036.!
However,!the!economic!growth!rate!target!is!also!driven!by!the!need!to!generate!
sufficient!jobs!to!achieve!full!employment!(see!discussion!under!FOI!#!12).!!A!growth!
rate!of!3.7%!a!year!would!be!insufficient!to!achieve!this;!instead,!a!higher!growth!rate!of!
6%!a!year!would!be!necessary.!It!is!proposed!that!this!higher,!more!demanding!growth!
target!be!adopted.!This!would!lead!to!the!achievement!of!highLincome!status!by!2023.!!

Appendix*2:*Economic*Growth,*Unemployment*and*Employment*Elasticity*
Introduction*
Achieving!unemployment!targets!(FOI!#!12)!depends!on!both!the!overall!growth!rate!of!
the!economy!(real!GDP!growth)!and!the!employment!elasticity!of!growth.!The!higher!the!
latter,!the!greater!the!number!of!jobs!created!for!a!given!rate!of!economic!growth.!!To!
achieve!a!given!target!for!job!creation!(and!reduction!of!unemployment),!a!higher!
economic!growth!rate!will!be!required!the!lower!the!employment!elasticity!of!growth!–!
i.e.!there!is!an!inverse!relationship!between!the!two.!!

Achieving*Full*Employment*by*2036*
Recent!surveys!indicate!that!Botswana’s!unemployment!rate!is!around!20%.!While!this!
is!in!accordance!with!ILO!definitions,!there!is!also!believed!to!be!some!additional!
“discouraged”!unemployment!(people!who!have!given!up!looking!for!work,!and!who!are!
therefore!no!longer!in!the!labour!force!according!to!ILO!definitions),!and!underL
employment!(particularly!in!agriculture).!!
With!this!starting!point,!and!assuming!that!the!labour!force!will!grow!by!2%!a!year,!
employment!will!have!to!grow!by!3.0%!a!year!to!achieve!full!employment!(defined!as!
unemployment!of!around!5%!or!less)!by!2036.!

Employment*Elasticity*
One!of!Botswana’s!structural!economic!weaknesses!has!been!a!much!slower!rate!of!job!
creation!(at!least!in!the!formal!sector)!than!the!rate!of!economic!growth.!For!instance,!
over!the!decade!to!2013,!real!GDP!grew!by!57%!while!formal!employment!grew!by!only!
20%.!The!formal!sector!employment!elasticity!of!growth!was!therefore!0.36.!

Appendix*3:*Foodgrain*Consumption*and*Production*
Data!on!local!production!of!foodgrains!is!available!from!two!sources:!the!annual!
Agricultural!Survey!(from!Statistics!Botswana/Agricultural!Statistics!Section!of!the!
Ministry!of!Agriculture)!and!the!Department!of!Crop!Production!of!the!MoA.!The!two!
sets!of!data!are!not!entirely!consistent!with!each!other,!although!in!most!years!the!
differences!are!not!excessive.!Data!from!DoCP!has!been!used!in!this!exercise.!Local!
production!varies!considerably!from!year!to!year,!depending!on!both!climatic!conditions!
and!the!nature!of!support!schemes!in!place.!!Data!on!imports!of!all!foodgrains!are!
available!from!Statistics!Botswana.!!
Total!annual!consumption!of!foodgrains!can!be!derived!by!adding!together!data!on!
domestic!production!and!imports.!!!Table!11!below!shows!information!on!production,!
imports!and!total!consumption!of!major!food!grains.!Over!the!six!year!period!from!2009!
to!2014,!domestic!production!met!approximately!15%!of!total!demand!for!foodgrains,!
although!this!varied!across!commodities,!from!around!50%!of!sorghum,!just!under!10%!
for!maize,!and!virtually!zero!for!other!foodgrains.!It!also!varied!from!year!to!year.!!!
The!MoA!sets!annual!objectives!for!local!grain!production!based!on!the!target!of!meeting!
30%!of!total!cereal!demand!from!local!sources.!In!NDP10,!the!MoA!had!an!objective!of!
increasing!local!production!of!cereals,!oil!seeds!and!legumes!from!a!baseline!of!15%!of!
national!demand!to!50%!by!the!end!of!the!plan!(2015/16),!which!will!not!be!achieved.!
In!terms!of!plans!for!increasing!output!of!foodgrains,!it!is!noted!that!sorghum!is!more!
suited!to!Botswana’s!climatic!conditions,!as!it!is!more!droughtLresistant!than!maize,!
although!it!is!more!vulnerable!to!pests!such!as!quelea!birds.!Nevertheless,!with!global!
warming!expected!to!lead!to!higher!average!temperatures!and!more!volatility!in!annual!
rainfall!around!a!lower!annual!average,!the!climatic!preference!for!sorghum!is!likely!to!
persist,!and!hence!it!is!likely!to!be!easier!to!increase!sorghum!production!than!maize.!
Furthermore,!it!is!assumed!that!maize!and!sorghum!production!will!continue!to!be!
rainfed,!as!irrigated!production!will!not!be!costLeffective!in!Botswana’s!environment!of!
water!scarcity.!Finally,!domestic!production!needs!to!be!costLcompetitive!with!imports.!!!!!

government;!transportation;!and!intermediate!consumption!(transformation!and!
