TOP 10 ACTION/ATTENTION- FOR CMU/SECTOR LEADERS/TASK TEAM LEADERS
  1. FY14 Lending Milestone update and submission of documents on time are prime
     priority. Task teams are requested to adhere due date of submission to various
     reviewers/approvers. If a significant date is slipped, Board slot may be taken away. (see
     page 17). As soon any milestone is met, it is important to mark it actual in the system.

     order to achieve targeted disbursement ratio, the pace of disbursement needs to be
     maintained. Uganda Disbursement needs to pick up. SL/TTLs need to pay attention to
     slow disbursing projects.
  3. Project which are closing in next 12 months (see page 15) need a careful review of
     undisbursed balance. Task Leaders are requested to notify CMU on possible
     cancellation, so that amounts can be recommitted to the country IDA (this is the last
     year of IDA 16). If closing date extension is planned, please process extension ASAP.
  4. Projects at risk for Tanzania are 17.4%, SL/TTLs need to make candid assessment of ISR
     rating, especially review long term MS rating projects. Uganda project at risk rating is
     57.1%, TTLs are requested to take proactive measure to bring back the projects on track.
  5. One project is awaiting effectiveness i. e. UG-Competitive. & Enterprise. Dev. Project
     (app on 5/9/2013, signed on 1/31/2014). Task teams are expected to take necessary
     action to make it effective.
  6. End of Q3 is approaching, only 30% ESW delivered. 16 ESW and 11 TA are planned to
     deliver in this FY, of which only 5 ESW are delivered. (see page 18-19). SL/TTLs are
     requested to review the milestone carefully and realistically.
  7. AFRVP initiated AAA Cleaning up exercise. Sector Leaders and respective Task Team
     leaders (as communicated by AFTOS, CMU and SMU) to take necessary action to
     close/update milestone. The deadline is March 21, 2014.
  8. There are 7 TFs in Tanzania, 16 TFs in Uganda and 6 TFs in Burundi have Zero
     Disbursement in FY14. End-Q3 is approaching. TTLs are requested to take action
  9. TF Reporting - Overdue GRM/ICM– 6 in Tanzania, 2 in Uganda and 1 in Burundi. TTLs
     are requested to submit the Overdue Reports ASAP. SLs – kindly follow up with TTL. (see
     page 30).
  10. Review WPA burn rate (see WPA budget table). Some Sector Teams and Tanzania and
      Uganda country Offices have high burn rate, some sector units, including Procurement
      have very low burn rate. Overall burn rate is 64.3% (see page 24-32)
