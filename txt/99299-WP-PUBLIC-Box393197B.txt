3. Recurrent Cost Financing Requirements

A new Financing Strategy for FY1394 is not yet in place. An interim arrangement has, however,
been agreed to allow ARTF funding to proceed within select priority areas, including Recurrent
Cost baseline financing and the Incentive Program. The Interim Arrangement established that
baseline financing continues at the FY1393 level of US$125 million, disbursed in tranches on a
quarterly basis.
The FY1394 opening balance of US$265.44 million consists of US$100 million, US$125 million in
baseline ceiling and a remaining balance of US$39.44 million in undisbursed AHP funding. As of
March 20, 2015 a total of US$62 million has been disbursed, which constitutes two quarterly
tranches of baseline recurrent cost financing.
