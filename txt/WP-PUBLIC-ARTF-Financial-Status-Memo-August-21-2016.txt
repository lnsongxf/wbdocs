3. Recurrent Cost Financing Requirements

The new ARTF Financing Strategy 1394-1396 establishes that baseline financing will reduce to the
level of US$100 million for FY1395, disbursed in quarterly tranches.
The FY1395 opening balance in the Recurrent Cost Window trust fund of US$151.75 million
consisted of US$100 million as a buffer, US$25 million as the baseline ceiling for the first quarter
and a remaining balance of US$26.75 million in undisbursed AHP funding. As of August 21, 2016
a total of US$291.36 million has been disbursed, which constitutes 2 quarterly tranches of baseline
recurrent cost financing, AHP payment of US$80.66 million, and US$160.7 million related to
FY1394 IP Benchmarks.
