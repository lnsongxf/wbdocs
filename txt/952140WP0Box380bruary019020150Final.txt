2. Consolidated ARTF Sources and Uses of Funds for FY1394

Table 2 presents sources and uses of funds for FY1394 on a consolidated basis as of February 19,
2015. The sources of funds are net donor contributions, investment income less administration fees
and the cash balance carried forward from SY1393.

As of February 19, 2015, the cash balance stood at US$1,647.41 million comprising: (a) US$231.36
million in the Recurrent Cost Trust Fund (excluding US$50 million in the designated Account); (b)
combined undisbursed balance of the ARTF active investment portfolio of US$900.07 million; and
(c) US$8.17 million reserved for the Monitoring and Supervisory Agent, leaving a net unallocated
cash balance of US$457.80 million.

Administrator expects that a new Financing Strategy or an interim arrangement will soon be agreed
to ensure agreement on recurrent cost financing for FY1394. For the time being, however, no
recurrent cost financing is released.
