3. Recurrent Cost Financing Requirements

The new ARTF Financing Strategy 1394-1396 establishes that baseline financing continues at the
level of US$125 million for FY1394, disbursed in quarterly tranches.
The FY1394 opening balance in the Recurrent Cost Window trust fund of US$265.44 million
consisted of US$100 million as a buffer, US$125 million as the baseline ceiling and a remaining
balance of US$39.44 million in undisbursed AHP funding. As of November 21, 2015 a total of
US$342.09 million has been disbursed, which constitutes 4 quarterly tranches of baseline recurrent
cost financing, US$33.60 million FY1393 O&M benchmarks and AHP payment of US$183.49
million. In July 2015, an amount of US$152 million has been transferred to cover Q4 baseline
tranche, US AHP payment of US$100 million and UK undisbursed AHP balance of US$41.47
million.
